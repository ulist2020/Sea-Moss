sea moss production

Sea moss is a type of seaweed that grows year round in tidepools and inlets. We grow sea moss for our drinks in an ecologically clean region of Saint Lucia. In the Caribbean, sea moss has been part of the human diet for thousands of years and has been used medicinally. We use this experience to make our drinks to help you live a healthy life.
